A tea pipe is a device used to brew or to prepare a beverage by steeping leaf or ground materials such as tea leaves in hot water that is then filtered.

There are several designs to choose from including kettle, teapot, mugs, cups and spoons. Some designs use one type of material, whereas others are more elaborate and made of several materials. Tea pipes are usually made out of wood, metal and ceramics.

Some of the designs include a lid to cover the entire top. Some of the tea pipes have a perforated lid which allows liquid to leak out and the other designs do not.
